What if you had your own festival app?

I want you to help shape the FestivalsApp, regardless of whether you are a user, artist, organizer or just interested. Suggest a festival, make the app more colorful or explain to me how the app can be made even better, simply via contact form, mail or on GitHub.

The idea

I do some things differently with the FestivalsApp than with projects in the abnormal economy and refrain from displaying advertisements and collecting data.


In addition, this app is also an experiment: an attempt to see whether such a project can be financed through donations, be implemented in consensus with all those involved, and an attempt at radical transparency from the open source approach to project finances.

FestivalsApp, collective

I use GitHub to coordinate the project. The platform is aimed more at developers, but is accessible to everyone and allows me to keep track of things.

Transparency

All components of this project, including this website, are open source.


It is important to me that all processes that are necessary for the smooth operation of the app are open and well documented. The general project documentation offers a good entry point.
