Adiungo

Adiungo allows you to automatically mount your SMB, AFP and WebDAV network shares.
Adiungo on the Mac App Store
description faq

Once set up, you can mount and eject your network shares with a single click. Adiungo ensures that your network drives always stay connected. All settings and credentials are stored in your secure keychain.

Use mount rules

Create rules on the basis of network properties to determine when Adiungo should mount or eject a network drive.
